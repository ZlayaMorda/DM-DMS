## Mininmal requirements:
* User's role:
  * default
  * moderatore
  * admin
---
* Default user:
  * authentification
  * create, redact and del a page
  * add and del the tags in a page
  * subscribe a page
  * write, del the posts
  * add the emotions as a reaction
---
* Moderatore:
  * ban any page for the time
  * del any posts
---
* Admin:
  * as the moderatore + ban any user
